# ReliantAI — AI-Powered Websites for Home Service Businesses

## About
ReliantAI generates high-converting, SEO-optimized websites for HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, painting, and landscaping businesses. Each site is built with Next.js ISR, comprehensive JSON-LD structured data, and AI-first content architecture.

## Website
https://reliantai.org

## Products
- AI Website Generator: Automatically generates complete, SEO-optimized websites for home service businesses
- Template Studio: 6 production-ready templates (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Roofing, Painting, Landscaping)
- ISR Rendering: Server-side rendered pages with hourly revalidation
- JSON-LD Structured Data: LocalBusiness, FAQPage, Service, Organization, WebSite schema
