# ReliantAI Mobile App Design Proposal

The ReliantAI mobile app aims to put the power of an "Autonomous Growth Engine" into the hands of service business owners and managers. It focuses on lead discovery, automated engagement, and real-time conversion tracking.

## Core Screens

1.  **Dashboard (The Growth Engine Overview):**
    *   High-level metrics: New leads today, conversion rate, and revenue generated.
    *   Activity feed: Real-time updates on automated engagements and new lead discoveries.
    *   Quick actions: Start a new lead scan, view active campaigns.

2.  **Lead Discovery (Intelligent Scanning):**
    *   Map-based or list-based view of newly discovered potential customers in the local area.
    *   Quality scores for each lead based on multi-factor analysis (from the "Discover" phase).
    *   Ability to drill down into lead details (website, reviews, current presence).

3.  **Campaigns & Automation (The "Engage" Phase):**
    *   Management of multi-channel outreach campaigns (Email, SMS, Social).
    *   Automated sequence visualization.
    *   Status of ongoing nurtures and follow-up sequences.

4.  **Conversions & Performance (The "Convert" Phase):**
    *   Detailed view of leads that have converted into customers.
    *   Revenue attribution and ROI tracking.
    *   Performance metrics for different outreach templates and strategies.

5.  **Settings & Microservices Management:**
    *   Configuration for the 20+ purpose-built microservices (Citadel, CyberArchitect, Backstage, etc.).
    *   System health monitoring and API key management.

## Visual Direction
*   **Theme:** Dark mode by default for a sleek, "Command Center" feel.
*   **Primary Color:** ReliantAI Orange (#FF6E00) for CTA and highlights.
*   **Secondary Color:** Deep Slate (#051424) for backgrounds.
*   **Typography:** Space Grotesk for a modern, tech-forward look.
