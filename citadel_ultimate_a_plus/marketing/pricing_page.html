Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes. The core Citadel code is MIT licensed open source. You can clone it, modify it, and run it forever at zero cost. We make money by offering setup services and support for agencies who want a turnkey solution.

Do I need to be technical?

For the Self-Hosted plan, yes—you should be comfortable with Python, Docker, and the command line. For Agency Setup, we handle everything. Your team just logs into the dashboard.

What about deliverability?

Citadel generates .eml files or sends via SMTP (using your Mailgun, SendGrid, or AWS SES account). We don't provide IP warmup or deliverability consulting—that's where tools like Instantly still have value.

Can I customize for my vertical?

Absolutely. Citadel is built to be forked. Add your vertical to target_verticals.json, customize the services list, and modify the email templates. The Agency Setup includes this customization.

Is my data safe?

With Citadel, your data never leaves your machine (or your server). No cloud databases. No third-party analytics. Just SQLite files you can back up, encrypt, or delete at any time.

What happens if I outgrow SQLite?

Citadel uses standard SQL. Migrating to Postgres is straightforward—we document the process, and Enterprise plans include migration assistance.
