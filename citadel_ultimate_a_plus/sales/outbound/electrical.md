## Discovery Call Opener (20 min)

Use this structure:
- 5 min: current lead-gen and outbound workflow
- 5 min: conversion bottlenecks and response times
- 5 min: quick product walkthrough
- 5 min: pilot scope and decision criteria

## Discovery Questions

- How many outbound targets do you touch per week today?
- What reply/meeting rate are you currently seeing?
- Where do leads die most often in your current process?
- What is your typical deal or ticket size?
- Who owns follow-up and pipeline visibility right now?

## Objection Responses

`"We already do outreach."`
- Makes sense. We usually slot in where execution is inconsistent: qualification discipline, structured messaging, and traceable funnel outcomes.

`"We don't want another tool."`
- Understood. Pilot is scoped and time-boxed; the goal is to prove measurable signal quickly, then decide whether to expand.

`"Not now."`
- No pressure. If helpful, I can send a one-page outline so you have it when priorities shift.
