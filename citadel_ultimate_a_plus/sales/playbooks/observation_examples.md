# Specific Observation Examples by Vertical

Use these to fill `{{specific_observation}}` in outbound emails. Pick the one that actually applies to the prospect's site — never use a generic placeholder.

## How to Use

1. Visit the prospect's website for 60 seconds
2. Check their Google Business Profile
3. Pick the most relevant observation from the list below
4. Write it as a factual statement, not a criticism

## Plumbing

1. "your emergency plumbing page doesn't have a click-to-call button — visitors on mobile have to hunt for your number"
2. "your site lists 12 service areas but your Google Business Profile only shows 3 — you're probably not showing up in maps for the other 9"
3. "your homepage doesn't mention financing options, but your top 3 competitors in {{city}} all do"
4. "your Google reviews mention fast response time repeatedly, but your site doesn't highlight that anywhere"
5. "your service pages don't have any pricing guidance — even a range helps convert visitors who are price-comparing"
6. "your site doesn't have a dedicated drain cleaning page, but that's the #1 searched plumbing service in {{city}}"
7. "your last Google review is from 4 months ago — review velocity affects your local ranking"
8. "your site loads in 6+ seconds on mobile — Google deprioritizes slow sites in local search"
9. "you're running Google Ads but your landing page is your homepage instead of a service-specific page"
10. "your site doesn't mention 24/7 availability, but your Google profile says you're open 24 hours"

## HVAC

1. "your site doesn't have a seasonal maintenance page — that's the highest-margin recurring service and it's peak search season"
2. "your Google Business Profile shows a 3.8 rating with 2 unanswered negative reviews — that's costing you clicks"
3. "your site lists AC repair but doesn't mention specific brands you service — brand-specific searches convert 2x better"
4. "your competitors in {{city}} all offer free estimates on their homepage — your site doesn't mention it until the contact page"
5. "your site doesn't have a heat pump page, but heat pump searches in {{city}} are up 40% year-over-year"
6. "your service area page is a single paragraph — your competitors have dedicated pages per city, which rank individually"
7. "your site has no customer testimonials on the homepage — the first thing a visitor sees is a stock photo"
8. "your Google Ads are pointing to a page that doesn't match the ad copy — that kills your quality score and raises your cost per click"
9. "your site doesn't mention energy rebates or tax credits — that's a major decision driver for HVAC buyers right now"
10. "you have 200+ Google reviews but your site doesn't display any of them — that's social proof sitting unused"

## Roofing

1. "your site doesn't show any project photos — roofing is visual and homeowners want to see your work before they call"
2. "your Google Business Profile doesn't have the 'request a quote' button enabled — that's a free lead capture you're missing"
3. "your site mentions storm damage repair but doesn't explain the insurance claim process — that's the #1 question homeowners have"
4. "your competitors in {{city}} all have financing pages — your site doesn't mention payment options"
5. "your site doesn't have a dedicated page for your most common roof type (shingle/tile/metal) in {{city}}"
6. "your last blog post is from 2023 — Google treats stale content as a signal that the business may not be active"
7. "your site doesn't mention your license number or insurance — that's the first thing a homeowner checks"
8. "your Google reviews mention 'clean job site' multiple times but your site doesn't highlight that as a differentiator"

## Landscaping

1. "your site doesn't have a gallery page — landscaping is the most visual trade and you're asking people to call without seeing your work"
2. "your site lists 15 services on one page instead of having dedicated pages — you're competing with yourself in search"
3. "your Google Business Profile has no photos — profiles with 100+ photos get 520% more calls than those with fewer"
4. "your site doesn't mention seasonal services (leaf removal, snow, spring cleanup) — those are high-intent searches right now"
5. "your competitors in {{city}} all show pricing ranges — your site says 'call for a quote' which loses the price-comparison shopper"
6. "your site doesn't have a commercial services page — you're leaving B2B landscaping revenue on the table"
7. "your site loads a 15MB hero image that takes 8 seconds on mobile — most visitors bounce before it loads"

## Electrical

1. "your site doesn't have a dedicated EV charger installation page — that's the fastest-growing residential electrical service"
2. "your site mentions 'licensed and insured' but doesn't show the actual license number — that's a trust signal you're missing"
3. "your Google Business Profile lists 'electrician' but not specific services — adding service categories improves your search visibility"
4. "your site doesn't mention panel upgrades, but that's the #1 searched electrical service in {{city}} for homeowners"
5. "your competitors all have emergency electrical pages with click-to-call — your site buries the phone number in the footer"
6. "your site doesn't mention smart home installation — that's a growing segment and you're already qualified to do it"
7. "your Google reviews are all 5-star but you only have 12 — your competitors have 80+, which means they show up first"

## Painting

1. "your site doesn't have before/after photos — painting is the easiest trade to show transformation and you're not using it"
2. "your site doesn't separate interior and exterior into dedicated pages — those are different buyers with different search intent"
3. "your Google Business Profile doesn't list 'cabinet painting' as a service — that's a high-margin add-on that gets its own searches"
4. "your site doesn't mention the paint brands you use — brand-conscious homeowners search for 'Benjamin Moore painter near me'"
5. "your competitors in {{city}} all show color consultation as a service — your site doesn't mention it"
6. "your site has no testimonials from commercial clients — if you do commercial work, that's a separate audience you're not reaching"
7. "your site doesn't have a dedicated page for deck/fence staining — that's a seasonal service with high search volume right now"

## Agency (for agency ICP outbound)

1. "I noticed {{agency_name}} manages outbound for several local service clients — it looks like you're running separate workflows per client"
2. "your agency site mentions 'lead generation' but doesn't describe the outbound process — prospects want to know how the sausage is made"
3. "I saw you're hiring a VA for outbound ops — that's usually a sign the manual process isn't scaling"
4. "your case studies show great results but don't mention the outbound infrastructure — that's a differentiator you're underselling"
5. "I noticed you manage both plumbing and HVAC clients — those verticals share enough DNA that one outbound system can serve both"
