# Qualification Rubric

Score each prospect after the discovery call. Minimum 7/10 to send a pilot proposal.

## Scoring Criteria (1-2 points each, 10 max)

### 1. Active Pain (0-2)

- 0: No clear pain. Happy with current lead gen.
- 1: Mild frustration but not urgent. "We could always use more leads."
- 2: Specific, quantified pain. "We're paying $90/lead on Angi and half ghost us."

### 2. Budget Authority (0-2)

- 0: Not the decision maker and can't introduce you to one.
- 1: Influences the decision but needs approval. "I'd have to run it by my partner."
- 2: Makes the call. "If the numbers work, I can sign this week."

### 3. Current Spend on Lead Gen (0-2)

- 0: Spends nothing on lead gen (referral-only). Hard to justify new spend.
- 1: Spends $500-$2,000/month on lead gen. Pilot is a meaningful % of budget.
- 2: Spends $2,000+/month on lead gen. Pilot cost is a rounding error.

### 4. Timeline Urgency (0-2)

- 0: "Maybe next quarter." No urgency.
- 1: "We're looking at options." Active but not urgent.
- 2: "We need more jobs this month." Immediate need.

### 5. Technical Readiness (0-2)

- 0: No website, no email, no digital presence. Can't run the pipeline.
- 1: Has a website but it's outdated or thin. Pipeline will work but observations are limited.
- 2: Has a real website, active Google Business Profile, some digital presence. Pipeline will produce strong output.

## Score Interpretation

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 9-10 | Send pilot proposal same day. This is a hot lead. |
| 7-8 | Send pilot proposal within 48 hours. Follow up aggressively. |
| 5-6 | Send the one-pager, follow up in 2 weeks. Not ready yet. |
| 0-4 | Thank them and move on. Not a fit right now. |

## Disqualifiers (Automatic No)

Regardless of score, do not pursue if:
- National franchise (Roto-Rooter, Mr. Rooter, etc.) — wrong buyer profile
- No website and no plan to build one — pipeline can't run
- Hostile to cold outreach as a concept — they'll never trust the output
- Currently in a long-term contract with a competitor — revisit at renewal

## Post-Call Logging

After every discovery call, log in the pipeline:
- Score (0-10)
- Key pain quote (verbatim if possible)
- Next step + date
- Any objections raised and how they were handled
