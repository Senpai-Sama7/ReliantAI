### 🟢 Kiva Microloan

| | |
|---|---|
| **Amount** | Up to $15,000 |
| **Interest** | 0% |
| **Timeline** | 3–5 weeks to fund |
| **Likelihood** | **60–70%** if you rally your network |
| **Credit check** | None |
| **Collateral** | None |
| **Apply** | [kiva.org/borrow](https://www.kiva.org/borrow) |

**What it is:**
Crowdfunded microloan. You create a borrower profile, invite your personal network to contribute first (even $25 each), then public Kiva lenders fill the rest. Zero interest, zero fees, no credit check, no collateral. You must not be in foreclosure or bankruptcy.

**How to maximize success:**
- Lead your profile with: "I built the software myself, I have the business plan, I need capital to launch"
- Include a screenshot of your Citadel dashboard — visual proof beats words
- Recruit 15–20 people from your network to lend first — this is the critical step. Kiva loans with early network support fund at a much higher rate
- Share your Kiva profile on social media
- Ask for $10,000–$12,000
