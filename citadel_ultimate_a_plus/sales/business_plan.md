## 1. Executive Summary

Citadel Outbound Services provides done-for-you outbound lead generation for local home service businesses (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing, landscaping, painting). We replace expensive shared lead platforms like Angi, Thumbtack, and HomeAdvisor with exclusive, qualified leads delivered through personalized outreach — at a lower cost per booked job.

The business runs on proprietary software that automates prospect research, lead qualification, outreach drafting, and pipeline tracking. This allows us to serve each client at high margin with minimal labor overhead.

We are targeting Harris County, TX as our launch market, with 4,000+ eligible operators across our six configured verticals. Our initial goal is 3 paying clients within 60 days, generating $4,500/month in recurring revenue.

---

## 2. Problem

Local service business owners face three persistent problems with lead generation:

1. **Shared leads are expensive and low-quality.** Operators pay $60–$150 per lead on platforms where 2–4 competitors receive the same lead simultaneously. Close rates on shared leads average 10–15%.

2. **Outbound is effective but unsustainable.** Most operators have tried some form of cold outreach (mailers, email, VA-driven campaigns). It works for 2–3 weeks, then dies because nobody owns the process, there's no tracking, and the owner gets pulled back into operations.

3. **No pipeline visibility.** Operators spend $2,000–$6,000/month on marketing with no clear view of what's working, what's converting, or what their actual cost per booked job is.

---

## 3. Solution

We run outbound lead generation as a managed service. The client does not log into software, manage lists, or write emails. They receive:

- **Qualified leads only.** Every prospect is verified for service area, active business status, real contact information, and relevant need before the client sees it.
- **Personalized outreach.** Each message references something specific about the prospect's business — not a mass template.
- **Full pipeline visibility.** A live dashboard shows every prospect contacted, every reply, every meeting booked, and every job closed. Weekly metrics reports show exact cost-per-booked-job.
- **No shared leads.** Every lead is exclusive to the client. No bidding, no competition.

Our proprietary system (Citadel) automates the research, qualification, outreach generation, and tracking steps. This means we can deliver the service with approximately 2–3 hours of operator time per client per week, compared to 10–15 hours for a manual outbound process.

## 9. Competitive Advantage

1. **Proprietary system.** Our software automates the most time-consuming parts of outbound (research, qualification, outreach drafting, tracking). Competitors do this manually or with generic tools stitched together.

2. **Vertical specialization.** We are configured for 6 specific home service verticals with pre-built messaging, qualification criteria, and market data. Generalist agencies start from scratch for each client.

3. **Full pipeline transparency.** Clients see a live dashboard with every lead, every touchpoint, and every outcome. Most agencies send a monthly PDF report and hope you don't ask questions.

4. **No shared leads.** Unlike Angi/Thumbtack/HomeAdvisor, every lead is exclusive. The client is the only company contacting that prospect.

5. **Low overhead, high margin.** The automation layer means we can serve each client at 85% gross margin. This allows us to price below agencies ($1,500/mo vs. $3,000–$5,000/mo) while maintaining strong profitability.

## 10. Go-to-Market Strategy

**Month 1 — Infrastructure + First Outreach:**
- Register LLC, open business bank account
- Set up sending domain with SPF/DKIM/DMARC authentication
- Begin 15-day email warmup (5/day → 15 → 30 → 40)
- Build prospect list: 250 plumbing + 250 HVAC operators in Harris County
- Begin outbound at warmup-limited volume

**Month 2 — First Clients:**
- Ramp to 25–30 first touches per day
- Run discovery calls, qualify with scoring rubric
- Close 2–3 pilot clients at $1,500 setup + $1,500/mo
- Begin delivering service using Citadel pipeline

**Month 3–4 — Optimize + Expand:**
- Capture real performance data from pilot clients
- Update sales materials with actual metrics (not projections)
- Begin agency outreach (second ICP) with proven case studies
- Add 1–2 clients per month through continued outbound

**Month 5–6 — Scale:**
- Hire part-time contractor for list building and QA
- Expand to additional verticals (electrical, roofing)
- Evaluate geographic expansion beyond Harris County
