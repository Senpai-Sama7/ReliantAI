## ✅ What WON'T Affect Your SNAP Benefits

**Good news:** Most of these funding sources **do NOT** count as income for SNAP purposes:

| Type | SNAP Impact | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| **Grants** | Usually NOT counted | One-time, not recurring income |
| **Loans** | NOT counted | Must be repaid |
| **Gifts from individuals** | NOT counted | Non-recurring |
| **Business revenue** | Counted after expenses | Net profit matters, not gross |

**Important:** Report any significant changes to your SNAP caseworker, but loans and most grants shouldn't affect eligibility.

**Verify locally:** Call your Texas HHS office to confirm: 2-1-1 or (877) 541-7905
