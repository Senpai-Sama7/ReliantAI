# Citadel for Agencies Managing Local Service Clients

## The Problem

You're managing outbound for 5-15 local service clients. Each one needs:
- A qualified prospect list
- Personalized outreach copy
- Follow-up sequences
- Pipeline visibility and reporting

Right now you're duct-taping this together with spreadsheets, a VA, maybe Instantly or Lemlist, and a lot of manual work. Every new client means rebuilding the same workflow from scratch. Your margins shrink as you scale because the labor doesn't.

## What Citadel Does for Agencies

One system that runs the entire outbound pipeline per client:

1. Point it at a target market (vertical + geography)
2. It scouts and qualifies prospects automatically
3. Generates personalized outreach with structured 7-beat messaging
4. Tracks every lead from first touch → reply → meeting → close
5. Gives each client a dashboard showing their funnel in real time

You configure once per vertical. Then you deploy the same pipeline across every client in that vertical.

## Why This Matters for Your Margins

**Without Citadel:**
- 3-5 hours/week per client on list building, outreach, and reporting
- VA costs scale linearly with client count
- Inconsistent execution = inconsistent results = churn

**With Citadel:**
- 30 minutes/week per client (review dashboard, adjust targeting)
- Pipeline runs continuously without manual intervention
- Every client gets the same quality of execution regardless of team bandwidth

At 10 clients, that's 30-45 hours/week saved. That's either margin or capacity for more clients.

## Technical Fit

- Self-hosted: runs on your infrastructure, your data stays yours
