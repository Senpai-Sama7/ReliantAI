## 1. Executive Summary

Citadel Outbound Services provides done-for-you outbound lead generation for local home service businesses. We replace expensive shared lead platforms (Angi, Thumbtack, HomeAdvisor) with exclusive, qualified leads delivered through personalized outreach — at a lower cost per booked job.

The business runs on proprietary software that automates prospect research, lead qualification, outreach drafting, and pipeline tracking. This allows us to serve each client at 85% gross margin with minimal labor overhead.

We are targeting Harris County, TX as our launch market, with 4,000+ eligible operators across six verticals. Our initial goal is 3 paying clients within 60 days, generating $4,500/month in recurring revenue and reaching operational break-even with owner salary by Month 3.

This plan is designed as both a funding document and an operating reference. It should be updated monthly with actual conversion, churn, and margin data.

---

## 2. Problem

Local service business owners face three persistent problems with lead generation:

1. **Shared leads are expensive and low-quality.** Operators pay $60–$150 per lead on platforms where 2–4 competitors receive the same lead simultaneously. Close rates on shared leads average 10–15%.

2. **Outbound is effective but unsustainable.** Most operators have tried some form of cold outreach — mailers, email, VA-driven campaigns. It works for 2–3 weeks, then dies because nobody owns the process, there's no tracking, and the owner gets pulled back into operations.

3. **No pipeline visibility.** Operators spend $2,000–$6,000/month on marketing with no clear view of what's working, what's converting, or what their actual cost per booked job is.

---

## 3. Solution

Citadel provides a structured outbound operating layer as a managed service. The client does not log into software, manage lists, or write emails. They receive:

- **Qualified leads only.** Every prospect is verified for service area, active business status, real contact information, and relevant need before the client sees it.
- **Personalized outreach.** Each message references something specific about the prospect's business — not a mass template.
- **Full pipeline visibility.** A live dashboard shows every prospect contacted, every reply, every meeting booked, and every job closed. Weekly metrics reports show exact cost-per-booked-job.
- **No shared leads.** Every lead is exclusive to the client. No bidding, no competition.

Our proprietary system automates the research, qualification, outreach generation, and tracking steps. Delivery requires approximately 2–3 hours of operator time per client per week, compared to 10–15 hours for a manual outbound process.

## 7. Break-Even Analysis

**Fixed monthly costs (pre-salary):** $720/month

| Scenario | Clients Needed | Timeline |
|----------|---------------|----------|
| Cover operating costs only | 1 client | Month 2 |
| Cover operating costs + $4,000 owner salary | 5 clients | Month 4 |
| Operational break-even (all costs covered with surplus) | 3 clients | Month 2 |

**Break-even math:**
- Contribution per client: $1,150/mo ($1,500 revenue − $350 variable cost)
- Fixed costs + salary: $4,720/mo
- Break-even = $4,720 ÷ $1,150 = 4.1 clients → **5 active clients**
- At 3 clients (Month 2): $3,450 contribution − $720 fixed = $2,730 surplus (covers partial salary)

## 9. Competitive Advantage

1. **Proprietary automation.** Our software handles the most time-consuming parts of outbound (research, qualification, outreach drafting, tracking). Competitors do this manually or with generic tools stitched together. This is what enables 85% gross margin at $1,500/mo — a price point agencies can't match.

2. **Vertical specialization.** We are configured for 6 specific home service verticals with pre-built messaging, qualification criteria, and market data. Generalist agencies start from scratch for each client.

3. **Full pipeline transparency.** Clients see a live dashboard with every lead, every touchpoint, and every outcome. Most agencies send a monthly PDF report and hope you don't ask questions.

4. **No shared leads.** Unlike Angi/Thumbtack/HomeAdvisor, every lead is exclusive. The client is the only company contacting that prospect.

5. **Standardized delivery.** Onboarding templates and delivery SOPs mean each new client follows the same proven process. This keeps quality consistent as we scale and reduces capacity risk.

## 10. Go-to-Market Strategy

**Sales motion:** Direct outbound to operators → short discovery call → pilot offer → 30-day execution with weekly KPI review.

**Month 1 — Infrastructure + First Outreach:**
- Register LLC, open business bank account
- Set up sending domain with SPF/DKIM/DMARC authentication
- Begin 15-day email warmup schedule (5/day → 15 → 30 → 40)
- Build prospect list: 250 plumbing + 250 HVAC operators in Harris County
- Begin outbound at warmup-limited volume (Week 3)

**Month 2 — First Clients:**
- Ramp to 25–30 first touches per day
- Run discovery calls, qualify with scoring rubric
- Close 2–3 pilot clients at $1,500 setup + $1,500/mo
- Begin delivering service; capture baseline metrics

**Month 3–4 — Optimize + Document:**
- Capture real performance data from pilot clients
- Produce first case study with lead/reply/conversion metrics
- Update sales materials with actual numbers (not projections)
- Establish repeatable onboarding and delivery cadence
- Add 1–2 clients per month through continued outbound

**Month 5–6 — Scale:**
- Hire part-time contractor for list building and QA
- Expand to additional verticals (electrical, roofing)
- Begin agency outreach (second ICP) with proven case studies
- Evaluate geographic expansion beyond Harris County
