### ⚠️ 3. Hello Alice — Grant Matching Platform

| Claim | Accuracy | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| $1,000–$25,000 range | ✅ **VERIFIED** | RealBizGrants confirms $2,500-$75,000+ range |
| 40-60% approval within 90 days | ⚠️ **UNVERIFIABLE** | Hello Alice doesn't publish approval rates by timeframe |
| No credit check | ✅ **IMPLIED** | Grant platforms typically don't check credit |
| 50+ active grants | ⚠️ **UNCLEAR** | They have dispersed $50M since 2020, but "active" count varies |

**Reality Check:**
Hello Alice is a **matching platform**, not a direct grantor. Success depends on:
- How many grants the user matches with
- Individual grant competitiveness
- Application quality

The 40-60% claim within 90 days is **optimistic** without published data to support it. A more honest framing: "Apply to 10+ matched grants; expect 1-2 wins if applications are strong."

**Source:**
- Yahoo Finance: "Hello Alice Has Dispersed $50M In Grant Funding To Small Business Owners Since 2020"
- RealBizGrants: "Small businesses can win anywhere from $2,500 to $75,000 or more"

## Tier 2 Analysis: MEDIUM PROBABILITY

### ⚠️ 5. Amber Grant

| Claim | Data-Backed | Reality |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| $10,000 monthly | ✅ **VERIFIED** | Forbes, NerdWallet confirm 3 x $10K monthly grants |
| $25,000 annual grand prize | ⚠️ **UNCLEAR** | Not mentioned in current sources—verify if still offered |
| 5-10% approval odds | ✅ **PLAUSIBLE** | ~4,000 applicants/month for 3 grants = 0.075% base rate, but multiple categories may improve odds |
| $15 application fee | ✅ **VERIFIED** | Multiple sources confirm |

**Major Issue:** Sources show Amber Grant is now **$10,000** (not $2,000 as some older sources suggest), but WomensNet expanded to **three $10,000 grants monthly** in 2024.

However, the 5-10% approval rate is **mathematically questionable**:
- 3 grants × 12 months = 36 grants/year
- 4,000 applicants/month × 12 = 48,000 applicants/year
- Base odds: 36/48,000 = **0.075%** (not 5-10%)

Unless there are category-specific pools, the actual odds are much lower than stated.

**Source:**
- NerdWallet: "Three $10,000 Amber Grants for women are awarded each month"
- Forbes Advisor: Confirms $10,000 monthly structure
